Our Approach

Integrated Curriculam
We introduce children to various themes & concepts through multi-sensorial approach and activity-based learning. Our curriculum covers key content areas of early childhood including phonological awareness, numeracy, general awareness, art & craft, rhymes, stories, handwriting and more to ensure holistic development of the child.
Personalized Education
Each child has their own learning speed and style. Our education framework, with guidance from teachers, allows our kids to learn in the most comfortable & meaningful way that suits them. This makes them self confident & ensures they feel engaged & in control of their own learning.
Learning Centers
Our campus is setup with carefully designed spaces for kids to explore & improve their skillset. Our learning centres provide kids with opportunities to develop their problem solving & decision making skills. This also gives the teachers a chance to observe each kid's strengths & assist them on areas that challenge them.

Few Words About Us

Childhood is an exciting time of discovery and learning. Early life experiences in children will influence the development of their behaviour and social skills, their personality and their cognitive skills. This stage lays the foundation for a child's future.

At Little Feathers, we always strive to create experiences to make children feel comfortable, express themselves, have fun, stay engaged, and pursue their interests. We strive to provide a fair learning environment for all and honour different lifestyles of families and communities. We strive to inculcate good habits and moral values in our children. We put our children at the center of everything we do to ensure they get the best start in life.
