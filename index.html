Our Preschool Programs

The preschool programs at Little Feathers are designed to provide the best of both worlds from MacMillan and Montessori. We introduce children to various concepts through multi-sensorial approach and activity-based learning. Our curriculum covers key content areas of early childhood including phonological awareness, numeracy, general awareness, art and craft, rhymes, stories, handwriting and more. We ensure holistic development of the child.

Play Group

We make the child feel comfortable & safe by creating a homely environment. We engage them with age appropriate activities and give them individual care.

Age: 1.5 - 2 years

Nursery

The child learns about colors, shapes and objects through play and creatively designed materials. We emphasise on independence and ability to do basic things on their own.

Age: 2.5 - 3.5 years

Junior KG

We introduce them to numbers and alphabets. They learn about plants, animals and the world around them. Attention is given to language through poems and stories.

Age: 3.5 - 4.5 years

Senior KG

They start recogizing small words and communicate in good English. Addition, subtraction & various topics on environmental science are learnt in detail.

Age: 4.5 - 5.5 years
