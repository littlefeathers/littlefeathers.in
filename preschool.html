Preschool Programs

HomePreschool

Play Group

We make the child feel comfortable & safe by creating a homely environment. We engage them with age appropriate activities and give them individual care.

Age: 1.5 - 2 years

Nursery

The child learns about colors, shapes and objects through play and creatively designed materials. We emphasise on independence and ability to do basic things on their own.

Age: 2.5 - 3.5 years

Junior KG

We introduce them to numbers and alphabets. They learn about plants, animals and the world around them. Attention is given to language through poems and stories.

Age: 3.5 - 4.5 years

Senior KG

They start recognizing small words and communicate in good English. Addition, subtraction & various topics on environmental science are learnt in detail.

Age: 4.5 - 5.5 years
